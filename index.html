Who we are

ABRIS Design Group is an international consortium of innovative companies that brings together several high-tech brands, each specializing in cutting-edge fields. Our expertise spans the development and manufacturing of advanced robotic unmanned systems for both aerial and ground applications, as well as pioneering solutions in information technology and energy independence. In addition to our technological innovations, we offer certified, comprehensive service solutions that leverage unmanned systems for a wide range of industries. Our team is equipped to provide end-to-end services, from the operation of these advanced systems to thorough data processing and analysis, ensuring actionable insights and efficiency for our clients.

Our Products

Explore our cutting-edge products and services designed to enhance operational efficiency and drive technological advancements across diverse industries

Unmanned Aerial Systems

In-house development and production of robotic unmanned aerial systems for performing a variety of tasks, including aerial reconnaissance, monitoring, and video surveillance. Ultra-high equipment durability and compatibility with sensors from leading brands ensure the acquisition of highly accurate data in the most challenging operational conditions. Over 30 years of experience in the field of manned and unmanned aerial systems.
More details

Ground Unmanned Systems

Czech-Ukrainian development of universal unmanned vehicles for the most difficult off-road tasks. TAHA UGV brand solutions are designed for use in off-road conditions and for work in hard-to-reach areas. The versatility of the platforms makes them an ideal choice for a wide range of applications, including search and rescue, scientific research, reconnaissance, and more.
More details

Software Solutions

The unique IRIZI software solutions complex for ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance) combines advanced geoinformation systems with tools for project, incident, and asset management. The in-house developed platform optimizes data analysis and resource management processes, enabling users to efficiently tackle complex tasks in real-time.
More details

Energy Independence Systems

In-house development and certified production of autonomous power sources with a high level of protection. Portable charging stations are primarily designed with extreme operating conditions in mind. Titanium portable charging stations are manufactured in Ukraine and can be modified to meet customer requirements.
More details

Service Solutions

The service division of ABRIS Design Group provides a range of specialized services using unmanned aerial systems: aerial photography, security measures, inspection, and monitoring of critical infrastructure objects, such as oil pipelines, gas pipelines, power lines, heating networks, mining fields, and more.
